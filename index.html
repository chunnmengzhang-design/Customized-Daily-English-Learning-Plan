3. 口语练习：15 分钟

场景

团队希望两周后上线新项目。你认同“尽快上线”的方向，但你担心质量和测试时间不够，需要在会议里礼貌表达不同意见。

先回答这 4 个问题

  1. Do you agree with the two-week launch plan? Why or why not?
  2. What is your main concern about the current timeline?
  3. What alternative plan would you suggest?
  4. How would your idea reduce the risk?

说不出来就用这个模板

I understand the goal, but my main concern is ______.

I am not sure ______ is realistic because ______.

Could we consider ______ first?

I think this would reduce the risk of ______.

口语范文

I understand the goal of launching quickly, but my main concern is the timeline. I am not sure a two-week launch is realistic because we still need testing and internal review. Could we consider releasing the core features first? I think this would reduce the risk of quality issues and give the team more time to fix bugs.

录音要求

录 1 分钟左右，至少用到 3 个今天的新词，并且必须说出 concern, alternative, risk 这 3 个关键词中的至少 2 个。

5. 写作任务：5 分钟

今天用 5 分钟完成一封 80-120 词的项目时间线邮件改写。

场景

你在项目会上听到经理希望两周后正式上线。你想先表达理解，再礼貌提出风险，并建议先发一个小范围版本。

邮件模板

Subject: Concern About the Project Timeline Hi [Name], Thank you for sharing the launch plan. I understand that launching quickly is important for the team. However, my main concern is the timeline. I am not sure two weeks is realistic because we still need more time for testing and internal review. If we move too fast, there may be a risk of quality issues. Could we consider releasing a smaller version first? This could help us reduce the risk while still making progress. Best, [Your Name]

你的任务

  1. 先把整封邮件手打一遍。
  2. 把 two weeks 改成你自己的真实时间，比如 ten days 或 by the end of this month。
  3. 把 testing and internal review 改成你工作里更真实的两个原因，比如 security checks and client feedback。
  4. 把 releasing a smaller version first 改成你自己的替代方案，比如 running a pilot with one client first。

8. 今日问答

Q：我不想一直说 my main concern is，还有什么能换？
A：可以换成：My biggest concern is ... / One concern I have is ... / What worries me most is ... / I am slightly concerned about ...
Q：alternative plan 不知道怎么说具体一点，能怎么替换？
A：你可以换成：release the core features first / run a pilot with one client first / extend the testing period by one week / launch internally before the public release
Q：邮件里 realistic 还能换成什么？
A：可以换成：feasible / practical / workable。例句：I am not sure this deadline is feasible. / I do not think this plan is practical at this stage.
